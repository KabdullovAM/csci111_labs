My Hometown: "Semei"

Semeyis my hometown. It is very big city with a rich history. It is original name is Semipalatinsk, which means "seven tents". Because at first it was literally 7 tents on the east side of the river. Eventually it has grown into one of the important cities in the Kazakhstan. Today

One of the most famous project in the whole Kazakhstan is Semey's own Semei bridge. It was build by japanese engineers more than 50 years ago. It is a bridge of a paramount importance for the citizens of the city. City itself is divided by a river. This bridge connects east and west banks of the Semey.

One of the most famous characters from the Semey is Abai Kunanbayev. He is the first person to bring enlightenment to the Kazakh people. All members of the Alash party were reading his works. Alikhan Bokeihanov, Maghzhan Zhumabayev, Ahmet Baitursunuli and etc.

Additionally Alash party's HQ was in the Semey. Here over 100 years ago Alash autonomy was born

Also many rich people of the city put a paramount effort in order to support Alash movement
